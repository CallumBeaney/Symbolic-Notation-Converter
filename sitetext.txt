EXAMPLE:

    All n in Evens. exists p, q in Primes. n equals p add q
                            ↓
            ∀n ∈ Evens. ∃p, q ∈ Primes. n = p + q. 


DICTIONARY:

    ALL/EVERY   ∀
    IN          ∈
    EXIST/S     ∃
    EQUALS      =
    ADD/PLUS    +
